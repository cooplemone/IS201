Profits in the United States

Note that the state with the highest profit is California, closely followed by New York. Washington is third on the list of States by Profit.



Historical Profits, by Month

When comparing the profits from 2012-2014 to the present profits, it is clear that California has grown within the last ten years. It is also clear that California is most profitable in November and December of each year. It is likely that this is the result of hospital spending!



Segmented Profits, by Category

It is clear that California drives most of its profits through office supplies. They account for over $37,000 worth of profit!



Forward Looking Sales

Given the regressive analysis we have done, this is the forecast which we have generated for Office Supplies sales. You will see that sales continue to ride an upward trend and remain seasonal, with strong increases seen in the tail end of each year during holiday spending seasons.
